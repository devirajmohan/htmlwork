About

Welcome to A beginner's guide to Podcast recording, the new, official podcast from BBC, where students, teachers and education experts share their life experiences and craft on the art of podcasting.Join us every month when new episodes will energize and inspire your learning and discovery - providing tips and strategies for making interesting, engaging, creative podcasts.

Get to know the Host: Lu Gerlach

Lu Gerlach is an educator who constantly seeks ways to enrich the learning process in classrooms without breaking the bank. She holds a PhD in Educational Science & Pedagogy. I love sharing ideas that can be applied to any learning setting, because good practice can be used anywhere. She has a passion for making learning fun and accessible for everyone. Gerlach has years of experience in communication and education.
